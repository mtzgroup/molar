Privacy Policy

Last updated: Match 23, 2021

The MolAR app respects the users' privacy and strives to send as little data outside your device as possible. We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Here, we describe what are sent outside your device.

When you view a 3D model or a preview image of a protein or a molecule that is not pre-bundled with the app, the app sends the resource requests to our server. The server may send the requests to the RCSB Protein Data Bank and PubChem.

When you search for proteins or molecules, the app sends the search queries to the RCSB Protein Data Bank and PubChem.

When you the Scan feature, the app sends the photos to our server. We use Amazon Web Services and Google Cloud for image annotation. We do not store the photos nor write the photos to disk.

We do not and will never put any advertisement in the app.
