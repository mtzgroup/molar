MolAR: Augmented Reality

MolAR lets users visualize proteins and molecules through augmented reality.

Features

  • Augmented reality: Place a molecule or a protein in a real-world surrounding. No marker needed.
  • Visualize over 160,000 proteins in the Protein Data Bank
  • Visualize over 500 molecules from PubChem
  • Scan objects such as food and see molecules inside them
